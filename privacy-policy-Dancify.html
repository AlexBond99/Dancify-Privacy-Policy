Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 28-09-2024

Introduction

Thank you for using Dancify! Your privacy is important to us, and this privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Dancify uses the camera on your device to track your body movements through pose estimation, which is powered by Mediapipe technology. The data collected from the camera is processed in real-time and not stored unless you explicitly choose to save it.

Data collected:

  • Camera Feed: The app accesses your camera solely to track your pose and movements for gameplay. The live feed is processed locally on your device.
  • Recorded Frames: You have the option to save specific frames captured during gameplay. These frames are stored locally on your device and are not shared with us or third parties.

2. How We Use Your Information

The app uses your camera feed to detect and analyze your movements during gameplay. This data is processed locally and not stored unless you choose to save certain frames.

3. Data Storage and Security

Any frames you choose to save are stored on your device. Dancify does not upload, share, or transmit your saved frames to any external servers or third-party services. We take reasonable steps to protect the information processed and stored on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

Dancify uses Mediapipe, an open-source library developed by Google for real-time pose estimation. Mediapipe processes the data locally on your device, and no data is sent to external servers.

5. Permissions

  • Camera Access: To track your movements using pose estimation for gameplay.
  • Storage Access: To save frames that you choose to keep on your device.

6. Your Choices

You have complete control over whether or not to save frames during gameplay. If you do not wish to save any data, you can simply decline the option to save frames.
